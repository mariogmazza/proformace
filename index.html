About Us

Proformance Unlimited is one of the leaders in the muscle car industry. We build all kinds of hot rods engines that will suit the needs of nearly every car enthusiast. Our dedication to detail in our engine build separates us from the rest. Check out our reviews and take a look at some of our YouTube videos. You will spend hours watching them. All of our crate engines are hand assembled one at a time using only the finest parts available.

Every one of our complete turn-key crate engines we build are dyno tested to ensure durability and power output. With these standards we can assure you that you will receive a 100% perfect engine that will stand up to the test of time. People choose to have a crate engine built by us over building their own since we can customize the engine to your desired spec’s and it’s assembled by our highly experienced staff. So the day’s of back and forth trips to the machine shops, trial and errors and expensive mistakes are a thing of the past.
